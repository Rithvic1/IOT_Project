Who can attend the workshop?

Students, professionals, and IoT enthusiasts with basic knowledge of programming and electronics are welcome to join.

What should I bring?

A laptop with Arduino IDE installed, a notebook, and enthusiasm to learn! Hardware kits will be provided.

Yes, participants will receive a certificate of completion from Amity University Bengaluru.
